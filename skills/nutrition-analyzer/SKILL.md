## Workflow
1. Intercept human food inputs, water intake, or supplement entries.
2. Log values into the secure local dataset using abstract tool sockets.
3. Check entries against the daily checklist (Multivitamin, Fish Oil, ACV, D3, Joint Health) and water target.
4. Return an elegant local tracking summary dashboard.
